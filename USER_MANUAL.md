## üöÄ Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Visit the Homepage**
   - Go to the Emotional AI Support website
   - You'll see the welcome page with "Get Started" and "Sign In" buttons

2. **Sign Up**

   - Click the **"Login"** button

## üí¨ Using the Chat Interface

### Starting a Conversation

1. **New Chat**
   - When you first open the chat, you'll see a welcome message
   - Type your message in the input box at the bottom
   - Press **Enter** or click the **Send** button (üí¨)

2. **Sending Messages**
   - Type your message in the text input area
   - Click the **Send** button or press **Enter** to send

- **Real-time Responses**: Get instant AI responses to your messages
- **Message History**: See all previous messages in the current conversation
- **Scrollable Chat**: Scroll up to see older messages in the conversation
- **Auto-scroll**: Chat automatically scrolls to show new messages

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Problem**: Unable to log in with your credentials

**Solutions**:
- Check that your email and password are correct
- Make sure Caps Lock is off
- Try resetting your password (if available)
- Clear your browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser

### Messages Not Sending

**Problem**: Messages aren't being sent or received

**Solutions**:
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
- Clear browser cache
- Try logging out and back in
- Check if the service is online

### Conversations Not Loading

**Problem**: Can't see your conversation history

**Solutions**:
- Refresh the page
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure you're logged in
- Try opening the conversations sidebar again
- Clear browser cache if problem persists

### Chat Interface Issues

**Problem**: Chat interface looks broken or not working

**Solutions**:
- Hard refresh the page: **Ctrl+Shift+R** (Windows) or **Cmd+Shift+R** (Mac)
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Check if JavaScript is enabled
- Disable browser extensions that might interfere

### Sidebar Not Opening

**Problem**: Conversations sidebar won't open

**Solutions**:
- Click the menu button (üí¨) again
- Refresh the page
- Check if you're on a mobile device (sidebar may work differently)
- Try clicking the logo to go to homepage and back

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is Emotional AI Support free to use?**  
A: Yes, the basic service is free. Check the website for any premium features.

**Q: Is my data private?**  
A: Yes, your conversations are private and secure. See our Privacy Policy for details.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**  
A: Account deletion options are available in your account settings or by contacting support.

**Q: Does the AI remember previous conversations?**  
A: Yes, the AI can access your conversation history within the same conversation thread.

**Q: Can I use this on mobile?**  
A: Yes, the website is mobile-responsive and works on smartphones and tablets.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**  
A: Modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge are supported.

**Q: Do I need to install anything?**  
A: No, this is a web-based service. Just visit the website in your browser.

**Q: Can I use it offline?**  
A: No, an internet connection is required to use the service.

**Q: How do I report a bug?**  
A: Contact support through the website or email provided.

### Usage Questions

**Q: How many conversations can I have?**  
A: You can have unlimited conversations. Each conversation is saved separately.

**Q: Can I edit or delete messages?**  
A: Currently, messages cannot be edited after sending. Deletion features may vary.

**Q: Can I share conversations?**  
A: You can manually copy and share conversation text, but there's no built-in sharing feature.

**Q: How long are conversations saved?**  
A: Conversations are saved until you delete your account or manually delete them.

**Q: Can I export my conversations?**  
A: Export features may be available. Check your account settings or contact support.

### Privacy & Security

**Q: Who can see my conversations?**  
A: Only you can see your conversations. They are stored securely and privately.

**Q: Is my password secure?**  
A: Yes, passwords are encrypted using industry-standard security measures.

**Q: What information is collected?**  
A: We collect your name, email, and conversation data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
